You are currently in the room I grew up in.

A very comfortable space. Many games and puzzles were played on this floor.

The Main Bed
Always so many cuddly characters, piled high in the corner. There were also many cartoon, games, and trivia posters on the walls too.
The Secondary Bed
When you turn a bunkbed into a single bed, you're still left with a extra bed. Luckily, for a kid, this makes for a great play surface and convenient storage. You've found A Backpack. Very useful to carry items you need in life.
The Book Shelf
I had such a wide variety of books.
Cartoons and Comics Captain Underpants, Hank Zipzer, Judy and Stink Moody, Horrid Henry. All just fun characters that get into fun adventures.
Young Adult At a young age, I was a good enough reader that I read above my average 'weight class' you could say. I loved the Artemis Fowl, Gone, and H.I.V.E. book series'. I was so proud when I finished these. They would become a foundation of my interests in science fiction.
Miscellaneous A wide array of books in this category. Trivia, joke, and puzzle books. Coloring and 'How to draw' books. This group of books is the start to why I now just like learning stupid or fun new facts.
The Wardrobe
Built by a friend in the neighbourhood, this thing held a large assortment of things.
Bottom shelf : Toys There was a crate for action figures, a box of LEGO and various LEGO sets, and a big truck that held many Hot Wheels.
Middle Shelf : Jigsaw Puzzles and games I owned the most puzzles and boardgames in the house. Monopoly was a family favourite but I love my couple hundred piece jigsaw puzzle that I'd just zone out buuilding.
Top Shelf : Clothes Not much to say here... Clothes are clothes. I never really had a particular style. Not on the same shelf but I did large a tub of shoes and runners.
Open area : Wild card This was the wild west of my wardrobe. Anything could be in here. Though I had jackets and nicer clothes hanging on their rod, the shelf below was just open space. I was pretty bad at trying to keep my room clean and any time my Granny came over, anything that was not in its correct place she would just throw into that space and quickly close the door for me to find later.
