Covid Era

Me and my friends had funny party scheduled, planned for St Patrick's day party. Like most places, we were sent home being told school would be closed for about 2 weeks... 2 weeks turned into a month... A month turned into a ...etc. You understand where I'm going with this. So much for that party. Now the college scrambled to figure out how to run things. Covid changed my planned 2 years into 3 1/2 years Interesting thing about me though : I have a pretty high immune system. So when Covid got into our household, while my family were to tired to get out of bed or off the couch, I was just chilling in my room, gaming or on call with friends.


Gaming

Though Covid hit, it brought the opportunity to play games online with my friends. Thanks to Adam and his partner, Charlie, I got introduced to a great community of friends online. The world was locked down but we still had access to the internet. We made the absolute most of the tool we had. Gaming, streaming, long calls at night. It was a new time for everyone and everyone had there own way of dealing with it.
